Beerworking isn’t your typical social event or beer tasting experience. Born out of a need for networking, we create an experience where professionals could come together and participate in a fun social activity that feels organic and educational.

We’re a group of beer and spirit lovers, and marketers who know the power of promoting your personal brand. After years of struggling to network in “comfortable” situations where we weren’t comfortable to be ourselves we decided to confront the issue head on.

After our first foray into our Signature Mashup Series we wanted to build on that positive response. We love the Food & Beverage industry so why not work in it? Beerworking is our incredibly fun, creative project designed to push the boundaries of Beer and Spirit tasting creativity while providing an opportunity for everyone to socialize in a unique way.

Now more than ever young professionals need to meet people outside of work and expand their networks to succeed and advance. Our objective is to make socializing less awkward.

We want Beerworking to be a place where our friends, colleagues and guests feel comfortable and are drawn together by a common interest. We partner with some incredible brewers, distillers and caterers to bring you one awesome, educational social experience.

Who doesn’t love a unique networking opportunity at a conference? Or a signature cocktail bar at a birthday? Maybe you’ve got 10 friends over and you want to learn how to pair beer and cheese? Is your boss asking you for a creative team building event at your next retreat?

Doesn’t matter what style of private or corporate event you are planning; we are here to make sure that your social experience is stellar. We have a variety of customizable turn-key tasting and networking solutions that bring your party to life.
